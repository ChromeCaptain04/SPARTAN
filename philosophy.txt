- Effort is periodized such that deload phases are built into the program to ensure longevity. 
- The way the program is set up aligns with the method I used to achieve a 100kg bench press, specifically an accumulation and leaking phase.
- The main difference to my previous program is submaximal effort 

Three mesocycles:

1. Accumulation phase:
- Build muscle tissue
- progressively overload in the 6-8 rep range
- 10-20 sets per muscle group per week

2. Strength phase:
- progressively overload in the 3-6 rep range
- 8 sets per muscle group per week

3. Peaking phase:
- Heavy singles
- Only go for 1rm in the final week

Four microcycles:

Week 1 (conservative): roughly RPE 7
Week 2 (moderate): RPE 8
Week 3 (Hard): RPE 9
Week 4 (Maximum effort): Near RPE 10
